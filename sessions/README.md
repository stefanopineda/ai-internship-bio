# Sessions

Seven ~60-minute sessions (≈45 min of talking points each + Q&A / interactive exercises). Each draws on real experience and a specific reading list. Suggested teaching order below — the live AI build is the capstone, not the opener.

1. [I Knocked on 1000 Doors So You Don't Have To](01-sell-anything.md) — selling anything, from doorbells to DMs
2. [The Money Waterfall](02-personal-finance.md) — the order every dollar should go (US + India tracks)
3. [Heads I Win, Tails I Don't Lose Much](03-calculated-risk.md) — calculated risk & building optionality
4. [The Only Equation in Business That Matters](04-value-equation.md) — Hormozi's value equation & product teardowns
5. [Show Me the Incentive, I'll Show You the Outcome](05-incentives.md) — incentives 101: token metrics, Navy FITREPs, US healthcare
6. [You Don't Rise to the Occasion](06-leadership-under-pressure.md) — leadership under pressure, from a submarine
7. [How I Cloned Myself With Claude](07-build-ai-tool-live.md) — building a real AI tool, live (capstone)
